---
title: ArticleTags
description: Inline tag pills for article metadata.
---

Renders a list of tags as small pill-style labels. Used on article pages to show categories or topics. Accepts a `tags` prop (array of strings). Renders nothing when the array is empty. Includes `aria-label="Tags"` for accessibility.

## Example

<ArticleTags tags={['astro', 'tailwind', 'pattern-library']} />
